## How ranking "on top" actually works (the pillars)
1. **Technical SEO** — DONE (semantic HTML, JSON-LD, sitemap, robots, fast static pages, mobile-ready).
2. **Local signals** — Google Business Profile (GMB) is the #1 factor for "near me" / city searches. **Currently MISSING.**
3. **Intent-matched content** — pages/articles that answer the exact questions people search. This is the biggest gap.
4. **Backlinks / citations** — mentions from other sites (directories, suppliers, guest posts). Needed to outrank established competitors.
5. **Consistency + measurement** — publish on a steady cadence, track rankings/clicks.

## Priority content targets (start here)
- **Cost/price FAQs** — highest intent, lowest competition for structured answers. People search "how much" constantly.
- **Location × service landing pages** — Karachi (pools/maintenance), Dubai/UAE (MEP/sauna/water features), Qatar, Egypt.
- **Buyer-guide articles** — "indoor pool design Pakistan", "sauna size guide UAE" — attract and educate.
