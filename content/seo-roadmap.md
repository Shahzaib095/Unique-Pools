# Unique Pools — SEO & Marketing Roadmap (honest)
Goal: rank page-1 on Google for "pool construction Karachi", "swimming pool Pakistan",
"pool MEP Dubai", "sauna UAE" etc. — and convert visitors to enquiries.

> ⚠️ Reality: NO agency can guarantee #1. Rank = content depth + backlinks +
> GBP signals + time (3–9 months). We build the assets; you do the local signals.

## What's ALREADY done (strong base)
- Semantic HTML, Oswald→Manrope/Inter, mobile-first, navy brand.
- JSON-LD: Organization (home), LocalBusiness (contact+2 landings), Service (services), FAQPage (14 Q).
- Sitemap.xml, robots.txt, canonical, OG tags, custom domain HTTPS.
- Google Map embed on contact. GMB kit (posts + review templates) ready.
- 2 landing pages: karachi-pools.html, dubai-mep.html.

## Phase 1 — On-page depth (I build, this session)
1. **pool-cost-pakistan.html** — the highest-traffic query ("pool cost Pakistan").
   Detailed PKR ranges, by pool type, with a CTA. Targets "swimming pool cost Karachi/Pakistan".
2. **lahore-pools.html** — 2nd Pakistan city (expand footprint).
3. **+4 FAQ Qs** — maintenance, renovation, best season, indoor — grows FAQPage + long-tail.
4. Internal links + sitemap update.

## Phase 2 — Off-page (YOU, ~15 min/week)
- **Google Business Profile**: post 1×/week (use content/gmb-posts.md), reply to every review,
  add the 3 new Q&A. Goal: 2–3 new reviews/month.
- **Citations**: list on Pak/UAE directories (see content/citations.md). NAP must match site exactly.
- **Backlinks**: partner/supplier mentions, local news, Chamber of Commerce Karachi,
  Facebook/Instagram posts linking to the site.

## Phase 3 — Content cadence (weekly cron already set)
- Mondays 09:00 `unique-pools-seo-research` drafts a post + FAQ from trending keywords.
- You approve via WhatsApp → I post to GBP / I publish the article.

## KPIs to watch (Search Console, free)
- Impressions & avg position for target queries (30/60/90 days).
- Click-through from GBP.
- Enquiry count (form + WhatsApp + calls).

## Expectation timeline
- Weeks 1–4: indexation + long-tail (Karachi/PAK-specific) starts ranking.
- Months 2–4: city pages + cost guide climb to p1–p3 for mid competition.
- Months 4–9: head terms ("pool construction Karachi") approach p1 with sustained GBP + backlinks.

No black-hat (link farms, cloaking) — those get sites de-indexed.
