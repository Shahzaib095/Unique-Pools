Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know before starting a pool, spa or water-feature project with us.

What services does Unique Pools provide?

We design and build swimming pools (civil and MEP), saunas, spas, Jacuzzis, waterfalls, fountains, water parks and slides for residential, commercial and industrial clients. We also supply pool equipment with after-sales service.

How long have you been in business?

Unique Pools & General Maintenance was established in Karachi in 1989 and has over 25 years of experience in the pool and general maintenance industry.

Where do you operate?

Domestically across Pakistan — from Sindh to Khyber Pakhtunkhwa — and internationally in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, Sharjah, Ajman and Fujairah (UAE).

Do you handle the mechanical, electrical and plumbing (MEP) works?

Yes. We deliver complete civil and MEP pool works, including filtration, pumps, automation and plant-room systems. We have built 90+ pools (civil & MEP) and completed 100+ MEP pool works across the UAE.

Can you build a pool for a hotel or resort?

Absolutely. We have delivered hospitality projects including sauna and spa works at the Mövenpick Hotel, Karachi, along with resorts and water parks regionally.

How long does a swimming pool take to build?

Timelines vary with size, design complexity and site conditions. After a site assessment we provide a detailed schedule. Contact us with your location and scope for an estimate.

Do you offer maintenance and after-sales service?

Yes. We supply equipment from quality brands and stand behind it with after-sales service and maintenance support for both domestic and commercial installations.

How do I get a quote?

Send us your requirements via the contact page or email Sajwad@yahoo.com. Share the site location, pool type and any reference images, and our team will respond with a plan.

How much does it cost to build a swimming pool in Karachi or Pakistan?

A standard residential swimming pool in Pakistan typically ranges from PKR 3 million to PKR 8 million depending on size, finishes and equipment. Larger inground or resort-style pools can run PKR 20–30 million. Final pricing depends on design, site access and MEP scope — share your location and we will provide a detailed quote.

Do you provide MEP works for villas in Dubai and the UAE?

Yes. We deliver complete civil and MEP pool works across the UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Al Ain, Fujairah) including filtration, pumps, automation and plant-room systems. We have completed 100+ MEP pool works in the UAE and coordinate with local authority requirements as needed.

Can you design an indoor swimming pool for a home in Pakistan?

Yes. We design and build indoor pools for luxury homes across Pakistan, with attention to waterproofing, ventilation and humidity control. Share your floor plans or arrange a site visit and we will propose a layout.

Do you install sauna and steam rooms in the UAE?

Yes. We design and install sauna and steam rooms for villas, hotels and gyms across the UAE. Typical home saunas are 4–6 person and 5–9 m², with custom builds commissioned over a few weeks.

Do you offer swimming pool maintenance services in Karachi?

Yes. We provide pool cleaning, equipment service and after-sales maintenance for residential and commercial pools in Karachi and across Pakistan, plus ongoing support for our installed pools in the UAE.

Do you build fountains and waterfalls in Dubai and the UAE?

Yes. We design and build custom fountains, waterfalls and water features for villas, hotels and public landscapes across the UAE, combining engineering reliability with architectural finishes.

Do you provide swimming pool maintenance and renovation?

Yes. Beyond new builds, we service, refurbish and renovate existing pools — retiling, plant-room upgrades, automation retrofits and equipment replacement for both residential and commercial clients.

What is the best season to build a pool in Pakistan?

Work proceeds year-round, but many clients start in autumn and winter (Oct–Feb) so the pool is ready for the pre-summer heat. We schedule site assessments and civil works around your timeline.

Can you build an indoor swimming pool in Pakistan?

Yes. We design and build indoor pools for luxury homes across Pakistan, with attention to waterproofing, ventilation and humidity control. Share floor plans or arrange a site visit and we'll propose a layout.
