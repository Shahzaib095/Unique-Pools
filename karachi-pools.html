Karachi pool FAQs

How much does a pool cost in Karachi?

A standard residential pool in Pakistan typically runs PKR 3–8 million depending on size, finishes and equipment; larger inground or resort-style builds can reach PKR 20–30 million. Share your site and we'll quote precisely.

Do you handle MEP and filtration?

Yes — complete civil and MEP pool works including pumps, filtration, automation and plant-room systems, with after-sales service.

Which areas do you cover?

Karachi (Defence, Clifton, DHA), wider Sindh, and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa — plus the UAE for villa and hotel projects.
