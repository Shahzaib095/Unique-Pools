Pool investment guide — Pakistan 2026

How much does a swimming pool cost in Pakistan?

A standard residential pool in Pakistan typically runs PKR 3–8 million. Larger inground or resort-style builds reach PKR 20–30 million. Final price depends on size, finishes, equipment and MEP scope.

01

Small residential (overflow / plunge)

PKR 3–5 million. Compact backyard or rooftop pools — basic tiling, standard filtration, modest plant room.

02

Standard inground (family)

PKR 5–8 million. The most common home pool: 20–30 m², mosaic finish, full civil + MEP, automation-ready.

03

Premium / landscape

PKR 10–20 million. Infinity edges, water features, spas, custom coping and planting.

04

Resort / commercial

PKR 20–30 million+. Hospitality-grade, multiple bays, plant rooms, DE filtration and redundancy.

What drives the price

Four things move the number.

1. Size & shape — perimeter and depth set concrete, steel and tiling volume.
2. Finishes — ceramic vs. glass mosaic, coping stone, decking.
3. MEP scope — pump size, filtration type, automation, heating, plant-room build.
4. Site access — excavation, material handling and soil conditions in Karachi or beyond.
